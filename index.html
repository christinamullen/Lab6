Hey! I'm Christina, Interface Designer and Developer. In 2023, I was the lead organizer for Viking Hacks Hackathon. Viking Hacks is an annual hackathon run by the GDSC club at Diablo Valley College. The 2023 theme was sustainability. The participants were challenged to create projects that helped save the planet -in just 48 hours! Seeing the participants creatively come together to make cool products was a major 2023 highlight! We had an amazing team to help produce the event. Here are some of our memories.
